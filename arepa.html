Traditional Foods

La Arepa

This is the most classic and representative Christmas food in the area, just like Westerners eating turkey for Christmas. Made with cornmeal and pork, beef, chicken and nuts. Wrap the meat in a tortilla wrap, in a rectangular shape, 5 to 7 cm long, wrapped in washed and roasted banana leaves, bundled with wire, boiled for one hour, and taken out in the refrigerator. Put it in for a day, and then you can eat it after you have tasted it.
